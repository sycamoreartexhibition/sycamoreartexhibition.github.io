Animal Testing

Animal testing is cruel and inhumane. There is a complete replacement for one kind of animal testing and several kinds of partial replacements, but some laboratories still use the cruelest, most painful ways to discover their unreliable data.

There are many animal rights activists who think that animal testing should be stopped altogether, including PETA, Cruelty Free International, Choose Cruelty Free, Leaping Bunny, dosomething.org, The Pet Collective Cares.com, and aavs.org. Most big cosmetic companies support animal testing because they profit from it. The list is a big one, so I will mention the ones that are more recognizable.Including the Sephora brand, Walgreens, Dove, Irish Springs, Colgate, Crest, Axe, Arm & Hammer, Band Aid, Vaseline, Clorox, Formula 409, Oxiclean, and Windex.

I can see where the scientists are coming from. Animal testing has given them some good medical advances, including, blood transfusions, Insulin, Anaesthetics, hip replacements, and asthma inhalers. I can also see why so many people want to shut down animal testing once and for all. Some laboratories are cruel, and give the rest of the labs a bad name.

My position on the matter of animal testing is that it should go on, but only if the least amount of animals suffer the least amount of pain. After all, why use cruel methods such as the Draize Eye Test and the Draize Skin Test, when 92% of drugs that are safe and effective in animal testing do not make it past human trials because they are too dangerous or don’t work. I hope people who think differently than me will partially agree with me in certain situations. If animal testing was the cruelest thing in the world I think more people would want to stop it.

If you do not share my opinion, hopefully this essay has changed you to my side. If you already share my opinion, you can pat yourself on the back for not advocating animal cruelty in animal testing.
