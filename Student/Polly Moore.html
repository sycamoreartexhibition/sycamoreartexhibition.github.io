                             Effects on your health when eating fast food

        Do you like Mcdonalds? What about taco bell, Jack in the box, or KFC etc..? Any fast food restaurant can taste good but the problems it can cause for your health are really bad. Your mind can be affected overtime as well as your behavior and health (www.CNN.com).

        There wasn't always places like Mcdonalds around. In the 1950’s Mcdonalds was founded and it didn’t take long for it to become a mark in the average american diet (Washington post article 2003 Browlee). In 1951 the history of fast food in america was secured so that’s when it started and the problem will just keep getting worse if people continue to do nothing about the obesity problem and stop eating stuff that's not good for you all the time (www.jhampton.pbworks.org).

        We are all eating these unhealthy processed foods now and it is seriously going to affect our health and heart. Did you know that 610,000 people die from heart disease every year and 60% of those people were obese from eating fast food (www.ABCNews.com)? It’s a proven fact that sticking to healthy food and exercising is the best for your body and your health (Bring the food economy home Helena Norburg-Hodge 2002).  

        Fast food can be very dangerous and scary if you know the things that it can be capable of. Especially because there are people dying from it. A 2 year old little boy died of a disease called E. coli because of the hamburger meat at jack in the box in january 2010. E. coli is a rare deadly disease that only little kids really get and it happens when that cow that was slaughtered had eaten it’s own poop and that cow got sick so the sickness got to the kid when he ate it (Seattle times article Warren King 2010). Not only is it really affecting your health, but it is affecting you mentally too and how you behave. When you keep eating Mcdonalds for so long your brain gets addicted so when you stop eating it then your brain feels sad but when you start eating it again you are happy. It's becoming something that is addicting like drugs and you can't help it (Supersize me Morgan Spurlock).

        Is there a solution to this issue that is causing bad health functions, behavior issues, and deaths? There can be but no one has shown an effort to ever care about americans heath. It’s really sad to hear or even think that people are dying from this horrible food.

All in all, we need to remember that fresh is the best so sticking to unprocessed foods is better for everyone. Fast food is ok once in every while but not ALL the time. So if you are thinking about eating at Mcdonalds tonight for dinner or eating at somewhere with fast food just think it over and have a salad or make some dinner at home, anything is healthier than eating fast food every night.
