Foster Care

               Foster Care Is a place for children.  Kids get taken away from their caregiver because of the environment they have them in or they get abused or neglected. Children don't like to get put into a home where they don't know anybody. You won't have any  family to talk to or anyone that you know. It's hard to communicate with people you don't know. When kids go into foster care they don't go into a long progress they go through a short one. I think we need to pay attention to this because we don't know what the kids are going through and what they are feeling. They lose so much family, friend, and favorite teachers. They  lose 6 months of their education, it's takes a long time for their foster parent to find them a school. They have to go to a new school because if they get abused their old parent/guardian knows what school they go to, and the school won't know that they went into foster care. Some kids have to go to 12 schools per year because of the foster parent don't want to keep them, so they give up on them. It makes me sad to know that those kids are losing that much time in school. They stay delayed and aren't at the same level as their peers. I think that they deserve more education. They deserve to be on the level their peers are on.
