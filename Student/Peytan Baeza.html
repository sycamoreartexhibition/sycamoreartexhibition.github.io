Guns or Crowns

        In 2016 alone 1,120 people were shot and killed by police officers (killedbypolice.net 2016).  1,120 people to 63 police officers.  Many lives are taken a year because of how often police officers use their guns.  It has to stop.

        In an argument against whitney iskenderian I have that there are points in time where guns can be used to keep police officers and by standards safe.  They don't always use the guns at bad times.

        Police officers also go through a training that is about 16 weeks long.  That can be enough time for them in the field but is it giving them enough training to control their actions in a scary time?

There definitely is appropriate times in which an officer should be able to use there weapons.  When a police officer is in the midst of gunfire and the only way they can keep themselves and others safe is to eliminate the harmful person.  That is appropriate because these officers are putting there life on the line to keep our country safe and, that is understandable but are they always keeping it safe or are they causing more violence.

  These police officers are putting there life on the line to serve but they are also abusing their gun power.  There are times when police can abuse their power.  It’s just the way people are.  If you were a king or queen and you had the power over a town you would most likely abuse your power it’s just what humans do.

On September 17, 2016 Terrence Crutcher was given commands From officer Betty Shelby  a Tulsa police officer.  Terrence Cutcher was unarmed when he was approached.  Betty Shelby and the assisting police officers said that Terrence Crutcher was not responding to any of officer Shelby's commands.  Terrence Crutcher then approached his car and stuck his hand in the car window and one of the male officers reached for his taser and officer Shelby grabbed her gun and then she shot Terrence Crutcher who was taken to the hospital and later pronounced dead (CNN 2016).

        

Betty Shelby was accused with manslaughter.  Her trial her prosecution her jail time could of all not happened if she did one thing different.  If shed of picked up her taser like the other male officer instead of her gun.  

        1,120 people were shot by police officers in 2016.  A Lot of the non police officers fired a gun first and the police officers were keeping themselves and by standards safe.  They were simply doing their jobs.

        But what about the ones that weren’t shooting a gun.  Yes that person may be bad and may steal or vandalize but does that mean they deserve to die because a police officer is unable to handle their nerves so they shoot their gun which always seem to kill the person.  Why is that?  Perhaps it’s because of their training.

        When a police officer is trained to shoot a gun they're trained to shoot at center mass.  Center mass is the chest.  If you get shot in the chest you’re chances of surviving are unlikely.  Why are  the trained to shoot there?  Because the leg is a small mass to shoot at especially when  they are moving.  Perhaps if they had longer training that wouldn't be so difficult.

        Betty Shelby was the officer that shot Terrence Crutcher.  She said that she wasn't really aware of what was going on around her, and she didn't even realize there were other police officers that showed up (CNN 2016).  It’s because she was scared

        This is when shooting a gun is not appropriate.  When the officer is scared.  They rely on their weapons when they are scared.  They think that it is the only thing that can keep them safe and that isn’t good.

        I get that you're probably thinking they are not using their guns inappropriately that there police it’s their job.  It is there job so if that’s what you were thinking you’re correct.  But think about something else.  Think about Kings and Queens that abuse their crowns.  Think how if the king or queen were in a scary situation where they could just use their power given to them by their crown and just have that person executed.

Maybe just maybe our police officers are like kings and queens and their guns are like crowns.  And when they’re in a scary situation they rely on their gun/crown.  I guess when it comes down to it kings, queens, and police officers really do have a lot in common.
